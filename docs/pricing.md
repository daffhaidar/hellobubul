# Hellobubul Pricing Model

## Untuk Pet Owners

### Free Tier
**Rp0 - Selamanya**

✅ Vet directory access (cari vet terdekat)  
✅ Symptom checker (AI-powered)  
✅ Community forum  
✅ Adoption board  
✅ Health articles & tips  
❌ Telemedicine consultation  
❌ Premium content  
❌ Priority support  

### Premium Membership
**Rp29.000/bulan** atau **Rp290.000/tahun** (hemat 17%)

✅ Semua fitur Free tier  
✅ **Unlimited vet chat** (text, 15 min/session)  
✅ **20% discount** marketplace (obat, makanan, accessories)  
✅ **Priority booking** (skip the line)  
✅ **Health tracker** (vaccination reminder, weight log, medication schedule)  
✅ **Ad-free experience**  
✅ **Early access** ke fitur baru  

### Pay-Per-Consult (Tanpa Subscription)

| Service | Duration | Price |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Text Chat | 15 min | Rp50.000 |
| Video Call | 30 min | Rp100.000 |
| Home Visit | 60 min | Rp300.000+ (depends on location) |

**Note:** Vet dapat 70% dari harga konsultasi.

## Refund Policy

### Membership
- Refund dalam 7 hari jika tidak puas
- Pro-rated refund untuk annual subscription

### Consultation
- Refund 100% jika vet tidak respond dalam 24 jam
- Refund 50% jika konsultasi terganggu technical issue

### Marketplace
- Refund 100% jika produk palsu (dengan bukti)
- Refund 50% jika produk rusak dalam pengiriman
- No refund untuk produk digital (e-book, course)
